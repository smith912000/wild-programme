What the Inner Room is held in

Three Pillars. Two Languages. One Room.

The Inner Room is organised around two axes that match the rest of the work. Three stages of cultivation — Body, Mind, Soul. Two languages — scientific, spiritual. Members enter wherever they are, and the room makes room for the movement.

Honest limits

What The Inner Room Is Not.

Set the expectations honestly so you can decide. The room only works when the people inside know what they joined.

Not a course

The teaching lives in the tier pages. The Inner Room is what happens between the lessons.

Not therapy

If you need a therapist, please find a real one. Inner work is welcomed; clinical work is not the room's job.

Not a fan club

The room is held, not centred on one person. The practice is the centre, not the founder.

Not a chatroom

Quiet days are normal. The room is slower than ordinary servers. That is by design.

Not anonymous

You do not have to use your legal name, but you are a person here, not a handle. People remember each other.

Not forever, unless

You can leave any month. The room is for now, while now is when you need it.

Questions

Things People Ask Before They Step In.

Do I need to have bought a tier to join the Inner Room?
No. The Inner Room is open to anyone who has decided this is the work — whether you came in through a tier, the free Seven Nights, the open door, or somewhere else entirely. The tier pages are how the teaching is delivered. The Inner Room is what happens between lessons.
What is the difference between the Open Door and the Inner Room?
The Open Door is the free Discord — anyone who has crossed any threshold can step in. It is faster, more open, less held. The Inner Room is quieter, slower, organised around the three pillars, and held more carefully. Both rooms have their place.
What does "held" mean?
A held room has rules of conversation. People do not interrupt. Speed is not the point. The founder is present without being central. Questions are answered when they are ready to be answered, not the moment they are posted. It is closer to a study circle than to a server.
Can I leave?
Yes, any month. Cancel and the room closes for you at the end of the period. Come back later if you want; nothing is lost.
I bought Tier 3. How do I claim my three free months?
Your Tier 3 welcome includes the invitation. You do not need to subscribe; the room opens to you for three months from your Tier 3 purchase date, then continues at a permanent discount afterward.
Will my testimonial be used publicly?
Only if you offer it and explicitly agree. The room is held, not harvested.
Is there a refund if I subscribe and the room is not for me?
The subscription is month-to-month, so if it is not for you, cancel before the next cycle and nothing more is charged. The first month is not refunded; it pays for the room you walked into.
