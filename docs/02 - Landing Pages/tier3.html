Body Pillar — Tier 3 Depth
Body Modules 1–6: The Body At Depth
B1
Food At Depth
Past the basics. Precise eating that supports the parts of the brain most alive in the deep hours. Food as something the body actually uses — not abstraction.
Depth nutritionPrecision
B2
The Quiet Allies — Cycling, Not Stacking
A small number of allies the body recognises. How to cycle them so they keep working. When to step back. What to never combine. Informational, not prescriptive.
CyclingRight hour
B3
Reading The Body Over Weeks
The body's signals across weeks, not nights. The small interventions that reliably move them in the right direction: slow breathing, cold, salt, less alcohol. Using the morning signal as your steady measure.
Long signalSteady measure
B4
Fasting And The Deep Hours
Eating only inside a window — what it does to the deep early hours of sleep, what it does to the body's repair signals, and which windows suit the crossing work and which do not.
Eating windowDeep sleep
B5
Cold, Heat, And Useful Stress
A short cold immersion lifts the body's alertness chemistry and helps the night begin cleanly. Heat in the afternoon shifts the body's temperature in a way that deepens the early hours. Useful stress as a way of expanding what you can hold.
ColdUseful stress
B6
The Inner Clock — Deeper
The body's clock and how everything else hangs from it. Light first, food second. Why a clock running a few hours out of alignment is the single most overlooked reason the door does not open — and the small adjustments that put it back.
Inner clockAlignment

Mind Modules 1–6: What You Carry, What Carries You
P1
The Mind At Night
What is awake in the night, what has gone quiet, and what shows up because of it. The relationship between the daytime self and the night's material. Why the mind speaks differently when one half of it sleeps.
The night mindWhat shows up
P2
Settling The Body That Carries The Mind
A mind always under load cannot quiet enough to cross. The practices that calm the body so the mind can follow: slow breath, somatic work, simple ways of telling the nervous system the day is over.
SettlingNervous system
P3
The Channel Between Mind And Body
The body and mind speak through one main channel. When it is open, sleep deepens and the night receives you. The simple, repeatable practices that keep the channel open over weeks and months.
The open channelLong practice
P4
Meeting What You Have Refused
The parts of yourself you have set aside come back at night as figures — sometimes friendly, sometimes not. How to turn toward them, ask, and listen. Why this matters once the crossings become deep.
Turning towardInner work
P5
Old Wounds, Honest Limits
How unfinished things from the past arrive in the night, and how the crossing work can — carefully — be a space to meet them. Where the limits are. When to bring a real person alongside the practice.
Old materialLimits
P6
Bringing The Night Back Into The Day
The kind of awareness the crossing trains does not stay on the other side. It moves into your waking life — slower, clearer, more present. The practices that let that transfer happen on purpose.
Day-night transferIntegration

Soul Modules 1–6: The Long Crossings
L1
What Awareness Is Doing On The Other Side
There is a third place between sleep and waking — neither one nor the other. What is happening in the mind there, and what the crossing reveals about awareness itself. The state every tradition has named differently and described the same.

Building Scenes That Hold

The Figures You Meet — Deeper

Old patternsEncounterTier 3 only

When Time Stretches
Why time on the other side does not match time in the bed. How attention deepens until minutes feel like hours. The way of going further in, and the quiet edge where the crossing borders something else entirely.
TimeDeep goingTier 3 only
L5
Healing And Making, On The Other Side
What the mind and body can do for each other in the place. The way of carrying a question across and bringing back what you find. Real uses — for the body, for the work, for what you are trying to make.
HealingMakingTier 3 only

The Long Sequence

Frequently Asked.

Do I need to have completed Tier 1 and Tier 2 first?
Tier 3 includes Tiers 1 and 2 in full — you do not need to buy them separately. If you already own one of the lower tiers, email us with your receipt for an upgrade code so you only pay the difference.
How does the private session work?
After you join, you receive a link to book your sixty-minute call by video. Bring whatever you have — nights logged, things you have noticed, the bit that keeps stopping. Sessions are usually booked within three weeks of joining, depending on the diary.
What does "priority for the gatherings" actually mean?
Live gatherings are being planned. When one opens, Tier 3 receives notice and a booking window before anyone else. Pricing for the gathering itself is set separately — your membership guarantees first place in the queue and the earliest pricing offered, not free attendance.
What happens after the three free months in the inner room?
After three months, the inner room continues only if you want it to. Pricing is set on the community page. Tier 3 members keep a permanent discount thereafter. You can stay, step out, or come back later — no pressure either way.
I have never crossed before. Can I start at Tier 3?
Yes — Tier 3 includes the full foundation. You begin where any Tier 1 student begins, with the rest waiting for when you are ready. Your private session is available at any point in the journey.
Is there a guarantee?
Yes — thirty days, no questions asked. If you have worked through the first lessons and the seven-night sequence and it is not for you, email us within thirty days for a full refund. Because the private session is included, we ask that it has not yet been used before refunding.
