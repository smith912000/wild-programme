Questions

Common Questions.

Do I need to have bought a course to join the community?
No — community membership is standalone. However, the community is built around the WILD Programme curriculum, so members without the course will find many references to modules, protocols, and tracking sheets they won't have access to. If you haven't started yet, we recommend at minimum getting the free 7-Night Protocol PDF first.
How does cancellation work?
Cancel anytime through Whop — your access continues until the end of the current billing period, then stops. No lock-in, no cancellation fees. You can rejoin at any time and your previous role progress will be remembered if you return within 6 months.
What's the difference between the two communities really?
Discord is tradition-rooted and practice-first. Think guided meditation, dream sharing, contemplative inquiry, and ancient text study. The culture is warm and reflective. Telegram is data-first and protocol-driven. Think spreadsheets, hardware reviews, peer critique of your WBTB timing. The culture is curious and scientific. Many members use both.
How active are the communities?
Both platforms run structured weekly programming — something happens every day. As membership grows, so does the depth of conversation. New members are welcomed with an onboarding thread and matched with an accountability partner within their first week.
I'm a Tier 3 member — when does my free community access start?
You'll receive a redemption code in your Tier 3 welcome email that activates 3 months of bundle access (both platforms). After 3 months, you'll be prompted to continue at £30/mo if you wish, with a permanent 20% loyalty discount bringing it to £24/mo.
