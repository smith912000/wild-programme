Is this technical or is it contemplative?
Both. Plain language throughout. The course covers what happens, why it works, and how to do it. No buzz words. No mystification. No oversimplification.
How long does it take to get results?
Many people who complete all 7 nights have at least one threshold moment by the end. A reliable weekly practice tends to develop within 3-4 weeks. Some people succeed on Night 5. Some take 3 weeks. The tracking template helps you identify exactly what's working.

Nothing. No wearables, no supplements, no apps required. You need a bed, a willingness to set an alarm in the night, and the tracking template (included). Module 7 of Tier 2 covers optional hardware — but Tier 1 is entirely equipment-free.
