Frequently Asked

Straight answers.

I've tried lucid dreaming before and failed. Why would this be different?
Most failures stem from skipping circadian preparation (Module 2) and avoiding sleep paralysis work (Module 4). The protocol is sequenced deliberately — each stage prepares the conditions for the next. People who follow it in order and track their results consistently achieve first lucidity within 5-9 nights.
Is this science-based or is it spiritual?
Entirely science-based. Every module is framed in neuroscience, sleep physiology, and cognitive performance research. WILD is a measurable, repeatable neurological state — the course treats it that way. If you want citations, you'll find them.
How long does it take to get results?
Most practitioners who complete all 7 nights experience at least one hypnagogic or brief lucid moment. A consistent 40-70% lucidity rate typically develops within 3-4 weeks of regular practice. Some people succeed on Night 5. Some take 3 weeks. The tracking template helps you identify exactly what's working.
Do I need any equipment?
Nothing. No wearables, no supplements, no apps required. You need a bed, a willingness to set an alarm in the night, and the tracking template (included). Module 7 of Tier 2 covers optional hardware — but Tier 1 is entirely equipment-free.
What's the refund policy?
30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you complete the 7-night protocol, track your attempts, and experience nothing — email us and we'll refund immediately. We're confident enough in the system to offer this.

Yes. The £50 you pay here is deducted from the Tier 2 and Tier 3 prices. There's no penalty for starting at Tier 1 — in fact, it's the recommended entry point for most people.
