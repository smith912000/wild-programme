# Micropage 2 — "The Fraction"

**Status:** Spec for build.
**URL when live:** `wildprogramme.com/i/fraction`
**Persona served:** Latent-capability / truth-seeker / biohacker-adjacent / anyone who has suspected their ceiling is artificial
**Function:** Second parallel door into the WILD experience. Direct from the manifesto. Recognition gate. Funnels to integrated landing on Branch A.

## Reader experience — beat by beat

| Beat | Time | What happens | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0–2s | Black screen. Single line fades in centred: **"You have been working with a fraction of what is in you."** | Low ambient drone fades in (60-80Hz, same as Micropage 1) |
| 2 | 2–4s | A small geometric form animates below the line — a partial circle that completes itself by ~40% then stops, marking the "fraction" visually. Subtle. Indigo accent stroke. | Continued drone, no change |
| 3 | 4–5s | Screen darkens 15%. Second line types itself in: **"You have been told that fraction is all there is."** | **STRIKE.** Sub-bass thump (~50Hz) on the word "all". Sustained 200-300Hz note holds underneath. |
| 4 | 5–8s | Both lines hold. The partial circle stays at 40%, not completing. The reader sees the visual metaphor without it being explained. | Drone + sustained note continue |
| 5 | 8s+ | Recognition gate appears (same as Micropage 1). | Subtle high-frequency texture enters |

## What's unique to this micropage

| Element | Reason |
|---|---|
| **The partial circle visual at Beat 2** | Wordless illustration of the metaphor. The circle is 40% complete and stops, visually showing "fraction." On Branch A it completes fully — small but satisfying visual moment of recognition. |
| **Strike word: "all"** | "All there is" lands on the word "all" — the lie is the totality claim, not the fraction itself. The sub-bass strike on "all" makes the lie viscerally heavy. |
| **Branch A line: "Then you already know what is being asked of you."** | Most consequential single line in the funnel. It confers agency and recognition simultaneously. The reader is not being asked to learn something new — they're being told they already knew. This is the truth-seeking frame fully landed. |
