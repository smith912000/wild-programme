1. Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the WILD Programme collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What data we collect

  • Email address — when you sign up for the free 7-Night WILD Protocol or purchase a course.
  • First name — optionally provided at sign-up, used to personalise emails.
  • Purchase information — transaction records from PayPal or Whop, including the product purchased and payment amount. We do not store payment card details.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics (page views, conversions) collected via Google Analytics 4 if you consent to cookies. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics data.

How we use your data

  • To send the free protocol PDF you requested.
  • To deliver course content and community access you have purchased.
  • To send follow-up educational emails related to lucid dreaming (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To respond to support enquiries.
  • To improve our products and marketing using anonymised analytics.

Legal basis for processing

  • Consent — for marketing emails. You gave consent when you submitted your email address. You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email.
  • Contract — for processing purchases and delivering products you have bought.
  • Legitimate interests — for fraud prevention and basic business administration.

Third-party processors

We use the following third-party services to operate the business. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Kit (kit.com) — email marketing and subscriber management. Kit Privacy Policy
  • PayPal — payment processing for one-time course purchases. PayPal Privacy Policy
  • Whop (whop.com) — subscription billing and community access management. Whop Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. GitHub Privacy Policy

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for email marketing — use the unsubscribe link in any email).

To exercise any right, email smith@negotiorum.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain your email address and name for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe and have no active purchase, your data is deleted within 90 days. Purchase records are retained for 7 years as required by UK tax law.

Cookies

This website uses Google Analytics 4, which sets a first-party cookie (_ga) to distinguish unique visitors. You can disable this by using your browser's "block all cookies" setting or by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or sell data to third parties.
