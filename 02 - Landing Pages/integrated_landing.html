The Diagnosis

Most of what you are is hidden from you.

The version of yourself that runs your nights — the one your conscious mind has never been awake for.

The version that runs your defaults during the day, below the level you can see.

Both are larger than the part of you that you call "I."

You have been working with a fraction of what is in you.

And you have been told that fraction is all there is.

This is not a metaphor. It is structural.

The mind has access to itself that most people are never taught to train.

The potential is already there.

The door is locked because no one handed you the key.

The Threshold

Every night you cross a threshold.

You have crossed it tens of thousands of times.

You have not been awake for any of them.

The threshold is real.

The practice is to be there.

The Practice

Three things have to align.
None of them are difficult.
All of them are simple — and almost no one does them.

One
Timing
Be there at the right hour. Almost everyone misses it by hours.
Two
Awareness
Stay present at the edge. There is a moment most people sleep through without noticing.
Three
Stability
When the door opens, do not grasp. The grasp closes it. The work is to stay.

That is all of it.
Everything else is what makes that possible.
