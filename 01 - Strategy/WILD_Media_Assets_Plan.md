## Notes

- All infographics should be designed so they can be **shared on social media** (crop to 1:1 or 4:5 format) — each one is a content piece as well as a course asset
- **Exercise cards** should be print-friendly — students who print them and keep them by their bed use the course more consistently
- **Videos** do not need professional studio quality — consistent dark background, good audio (Blue Yeti or similar), clean framing is enough for this audience
- **Binaural audios** should always include a mono version (binaural only works with headphones — some students won't have them)
