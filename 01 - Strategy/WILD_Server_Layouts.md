**1.1 Server Overview**

The WILD Performance Lab Discord is positioned as an elite
accountability community for people using lucid dreaming as a
cognitive-performance and sleep-optimisation stack. Free members get
protocol guidance; paid tiers unlock advanced training, coaching calls

**1.4 Bot Setup**

**Carl-bot (Recommended --- Free Tier Sufficient)**

-   Role assignment: Create reaction-role post in \#start-your-protocol
    --- member reacts to choose Biohacker or Spiritual track

-   Automod: Spam filter, link filter, banned-word list for competitor
    names

-   Welcome DM: Triggered on join --- sends 7-Night Protocol PDF link
    and \#start-here instructions

-   Starboard: Posts to \#wins-board when a message hits 5 star
    reactions (highlights community victories)

-   Logs: Route all join/leave/bans to \#bot-logs

**MEE6 (Optional --- for XP / Levelling)**

-   XP system: Award points for messages, voice time, protocol posts

-   Level milestones unlock custom role colours (Dreamer → Explorer →
    Master Dreamer)

-   Leaderboard post every Sunday in \#wins-board

**1.5 Member Onboarding Flow**

minutes of joining:

  **6**      Day 7 --- free member             Bot DMs: \"You\'ve been here 7 days. Ready to go deeper? Tier 1 unlocks the Advanced Lab. \[Link\]\"

as consciousness expansion, astral projection, shadow work, and reality
